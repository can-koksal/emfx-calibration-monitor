Methodology

Expected depreciation expected_rate / spot_at_T0 - 1

Realized depreciation actual_rate / spot_at_T0 - 1

Forecast error realized_depreciation - expected_depreciation

Bias-adjusted forecast spot_today x (1 + raw_expected_depreciation + historical_average_error)

Trend-adjusted forecast spot_today x (1 + realized_trend)^(horizon / 12)

Shock-adjusted forecast spot_today x (1 + raw_expected_depreciation + shock_probability x shock_error)

Final blended forecast weighted average of raw, bias-adjusted, trend-adjusted, and shock-adjusted forecasts

Data Quality and Source Labels

Historical expectations are manually entered. Actual rates are manually entered in v1. The model is sensitive to historical sample size, source consistency, and the type of expectation being compared.

Supported source tags: Public Survey, Bank Forecast, Forward-Implied, Interpolated, Manual Analyst Input

Public surveys, bank research, forward-implied levels, and analyst inputs may represent different types of expectations and may not be directly comparable.

This is not a guaranteed forecast and not a trading recommendation.
